# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone who participates in this project.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces including the repository, issue tracker, and any community forums.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
